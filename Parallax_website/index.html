Monkey D Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, known as the Straw Hat Pirate Captain, possesses a contagious enthusiasm and unwavering resolve to achieve his dream of becoming the Pirate King, which fuels his remarkable journey through the perilous world of "One Piece."

Uzumaki Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki, the orange-clad ninja, overcomes his outcast status to become a symbol of perseverance and hope, striving to protect his village and friends while chasing his dream of becoming the Hokage

Kurosaki Ichigo

Ichigo Kurosaki is the orange-haired protagonist of "Bleach," a young man bestowed with the powers of a Soul Reaper, tasked with protecting the living world from malevolent spirits and hollows

Light Yagami

Light Yagami, from "Death Note," is a highly intelligent high school student who becomes a ruthless vigilante after gaining the power to kill anyone whose name he writes in a mysterious notebook

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager, from "Attack on Titan," embodies a character defined by unwavering determination, as he grapples with the weight of responsibility and the quest for freedom in a world besieged by giants
